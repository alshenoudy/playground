# Research Log

A weekly journal of intellectual progress — what you tried, what failed, what you learned, and what questions are open. This is separate from experiment tracking (that's in W&B) and progress checklists (those are in domain READMEs). This is for thinking out loud.

Write an entry at the end of every week, even if it's short. The habit matters more than the length.

---

## Format

```markdown
## Week of [YYYY-MM-DD]

**Working on:**

**What I tried:**

**What didn't work (and why I think so):**

**What I learned:**

**Open questions:**

**Next week:**
```
